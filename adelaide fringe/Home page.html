About Adelaide Fringe

Founded in 1960, Adelaide Fringe is the second-largest annual arts festival in the world, after the Edinburgh Festival Fringe.

For four magical weeks each year, Adelaide Fringe transforms the city into a vibrant cultural hub, featuring over 1,200 events across 300+ venues.

As an open-access festival, Adelaide Fringe embraces diversity and inclusivity, welcoming anyone with a show, exhibition, or event to register and participate.
